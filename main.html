EMD BASKETBALL

At EMD Basketball, our mission is simple: to Educate, Motivate, and Demonstrate. Led by Sammie Walker, a former collegiate athlete with a passion for developing players, we are dedicated to helping basketball players of all levels reach their full potential.

We understand that success in basketball isn’t just about raw talent—it’s about the mindset, the work ethic, and the commitment to constant improvement. That’s why our coaching philosophy is built on three pillars:

Educate: We focus on teaching the fundamentals and advanced skills that every player needs to succeed on and off the court. From ball handling to game strategy, our training is designed to empower players with the knowledge to excel.

Motivate: We believe in pushing players beyond their limits. Our approach encourages perseverance, discipline, and mental toughness—skills that will serve athletes in all areas of life, not just basketball.

Demonstrate: We lead by example. As a coach, Sammie Walker is actively involved in every session, demonstrating the techniques and values that make great athletes. We aim to inspire through action, showing players what it takes to be successful through hard work, leadership, and integrity.

At EMD Basketball, we don't just develop basketball players—we help shape future college athletes and champions. Whether you're preparing for college recruitment or simply striving to improve your game, our expert coaching will give you the tools and confidence you need to succeed.

Join us at EMD Basketball and take your game to the next level!
